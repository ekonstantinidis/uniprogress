About

Uni Progress is a website that organises your studies. Imagine a calendar based on student needs, where you are able to see upcoming lectures, labs, coursework deadlines and much more. Everything around student life.

Its friendly interface allows you to setup your account in a few seconds. All you have to do is follow our 3 steps. Sign Up, select your university, course and year of study and you are ready to go. Check our calendar to see when your complete timetable, including lectures, labs, coursework deadlines, examination and pretty much everything a student needs.
